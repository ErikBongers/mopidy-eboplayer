Multi-layout
=============

- A layout has many components that can be re-ordered and hidden.
  > most of them can therefore be visible at the same time.
  > if they are always filled and up-to-date, showing them takes no time.
- Some components are almost never accessed: backend config.
  > Don't pre-load certain components: allow them to be deactivated.
    > Deactivated means they don't execute the update function.
      > Better performance.
    > Activating them forces an update.
      > This only works is updates are NOT incremental.
        > E.g. the Scan status is incremental, but a blocking of update would not cause loss of data.
